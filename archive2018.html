Notes on the tracker

  • The visualization above shows all public ballots for 2018. The red line represents 75% of these ballots. If a player is past the line, it means that they would have been inducted without private ballots.
  • The dotted gray line in the bottom plot shows a running 75% benchmark.
  • You can isolate a player in both plots by clicking on their bar in the top plot. To select multiple players, hold Shift while clicking.
  • You can now zoom in to the bottom plot.
